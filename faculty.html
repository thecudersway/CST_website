FACULTIES

Dr. Anandika Sharma

Dr. Anandika Sharma is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, School of Engineering & Technology. She holds a Ph.D. from Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, Patiala, where her research focused on enhancing agri-food supply chains using blockchain technology. She earned her M.Tech. in Computer Engineering from Punjabi University, Patiala, and her B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar. With over 4.5 years of teaching experience, her research interests include blockchain technology, artificial intelligence, and machine learning, particularly in the context of food supply chain management. She has published in reputed journals such as Business Process Management Journal and Operations Management Research.

Dr. ANAM BANSAL

Dr. Anam Bansal is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, School of Engineering & Technology. She holds a Ph.D. from Maharaja Ranjit Singh Punjab Technical University, Bathinda, an M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from UIET, Panjab University, and a B.Tech. from DAVIET, Jalandhar. She began her academic career at Lovely Professional University in 2018 and has been serving at the Central University of Punjab since 2019. Her research areas include applied artificial intelligence, machine learning, and audio signal processing, with specific interests in environmental sound classification for assistive technologies, explainable AI in audio, and customer behavior modeling.

Dr. Meenakshi

Dr. Menakshi is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, School of Engineering & Technology. With 12 years of teaching experience, she holds a B.Tech., M.E., and Ph.D. Her research interests include DDoS attack detection, network security, and deep learning. She has published in reputed journals, including a 2024 article on Alzheimer's disease prediction using deep learning in Artificial Intelligence in Medicine (Impact Factor: 6.1). She has actively participated in various academic programs, including workshops and FDPs on deep learning, curriculum development, and data science conducted by institutions like NITTTR Chandigarh, Central University of Punjab, and IIITDM Kancheepuram.

Dr. Swati Garg

Dr. Swati Garg is currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Technology at the Central University of Punjab, Bathinda. She holds a strong academic and research background in the field of applied mathematics, with a particular focus on Partial Differential Equations (PDEs). Her areas of expertise include the homogenization of PDEs and optimal control problems related to PDEs, which are crucial in modeling and solving complex physical phenomena.

Dr. Surinder Singh Khurana

Dr. Surinder Singh Khurana is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, School of Engineering & Technology. He holds a Ph.D. from the Central University of Punjab, an M.E. from PEC University of Technology, and a B.Tech. from Punjab Technical University, along with a diploma in Computer Engineering. With extensive teaching experience across reputed institutions, he has served over 14 years in various academic roles, including 11 years at the Central University of Punjab. His research interests include cyber security, machine learning, and algorithmic trading. He has published notable research, including studies on sentiment analysis and drug review analysis in journals like SN Computer Science and Multimedia Tools and Applications.

Dr. Parvinder Singh

Dr. Parvinder Singh is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, School of Engineering & Technology. He earned his B.Tech. in Information Technology from Government Engineering College Ajmer, M.Tech. in Cryptography and Information Security from the Central University of Rajasthan (Gold Medallist), and Ph.D. in Quantum Information Processing from IIT Jodhpur. With teaching experience in government institutions since 2014, he currently serves at the Central University of Punjab. His research areas include quantum information processing, quantum computing, quantum cryptography, cryptography, information security, network security, and quantum machine learning.

Dr. Priyavrat

Dr. Priyavrat is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, School of Engineering & Technology. He holds a B.Tech. from Green Hills Engineering College, an M.Tech. from Himachal Pradesh University, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Dr. B R Ambedkar National Institute of Technology, Jalandhar. He has qualified UGC-NET, HPPSC-SET, and ASRB-NET, and has over a year of teaching experience. His research focuses on multimodal sentiment analysis, natural language processing, and web and text mining. He has published in reputed journals, including work on election prediction using social media data and a comparative study on sentiment analysis with machine learning.
