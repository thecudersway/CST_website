ORIENTATION PROGRAMME 2025

The Central University of Punjab, Bathinda is proud to host the Orientation Program 2025 for its incoming batch of freshers. This program is designed to warmly welcome new students, introduce them to the academic environment, and connect them with key support groups across campus. Through this initiative, students will gain valuable insights into university life, expectations, and the vibrant community they are now a part of.
