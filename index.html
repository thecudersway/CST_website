Department of Computer Science and Technology


The Department of Computer Science and Technology is a vibrant center of learning and innovation, dedicated to equipping students with a strong foundation in computing principles and practical skills. It offers a modern curriculum covering key areas such as programming, algorithms, artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, software engineering, and computer networks. Supported by experienced faculty and advanced lab facilities, the department fosters research, problem-solving, and creative thinking through workshops, hackathons, and industry interactions. With a focus on interdisciplinary learning, real-world application, and professional development, the department prepares students to excel in the ever-evolving field of technology and emerge as skilled professionals, researchers, and leaders.
